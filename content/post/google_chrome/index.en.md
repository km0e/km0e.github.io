### Shortcuts
- Open the next tab: `Ctrl` + `Tab` / `Ctrl` + `Page Down`
- Open the previous tab: `Ctrl` + `Shift` + `Tab` / `Ctrl` + `Page Up`
- Open a new tab: `Ctrl` + `T`
- Close the current tab: `Ctrl` + `W`
- Focus on the address bar: `Ctrl` + `L`
